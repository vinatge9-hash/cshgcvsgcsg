Our Story

Founded in 2021, Coffee Haven began as a small neighborhood roastery with a simple idea: serve coffee that people remember. We partner with small farms, prioritize sustainable practices, and roast our beans with care.

Our baristas are trained in classic techniques and modern brewing methods. Whether you prefer a precise pour-over or a classic cappuccino, we aim to deliver a consistently excellent cup.

Sourcing & Sustainability

Direct Trade

We build direct relationships with farmers to ensure quality and fair pricing.

Small Batch Roasting

Roasted in small batches to highlight each bean's character.

Eco-friendly Packaging

We use recyclable and compostable materials whenever possible.
